# User Guide - AssociationManagerSaaS

## Introduction
Welcome to AssociationManagerSaaS! This guide will help you understand how to navigate and use the platform to manage your organization's associations.

## 1. Getting Started
### Signing In
1. Navigate to the login page.
2. Click the **"Sign in with Google"** button.
3. Choose your Google account to log in.
4. Upon successful login, you will be redirected to your dashboard.

## 2. Dashboard Overview
The dashboard is your central hub. From here, you can:
- View your account summary.
- Quickly navigate to **Associations**.
- See real-time notifications about your organization.

## 3. Managing Associations
### Viewing Associations
- Go to the **Associations** page from the side menu.
- You will see a list of all associations managed by your organization.

### Creating an Association
1. Enter the **Name** and **Description** in the "Create New Association" card.
2. Click the **Create** button.
3. The new association will appear in the table below and will be visible to all members of your organization.

### Deleting an Association
- Click the **Delete** button next to any association to remove it from the system. *Note: This action is permanent and will be logged.*

## 4. Notifications
The platform includes real-time notifications. When an important event happens (like a payment status update or a background job completion), a notification will appear in the UI.

## 5. Support
For technical issues or feature requests, please contact your organization's administrator.
